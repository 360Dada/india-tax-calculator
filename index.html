Key FBP Rules

• Declare at start of FY (April) or within 10 days of first payroll for new joiners. Cannot change mid-year.

• Unclaimed amounts paid as taxable income in March payroll.

• Mobile: connection in your name; company SIM holders ineligible.

• LTA: economy national carrier; 2 claims per 4-year block; food and shopping not covered.

• CEA and HEA: submit bills monthly; unsubmitted claims become taxable.

• Car Lease: verify eligibility with HR; car must be 5 years old or newer.

• NPS: employer must actively contribute to NPS. Confirm before selecting.

• Loss of pay in any month reduces FBP entitlement proportionally for that month.
