## 💬 What is this?

You get your offer letter. You see a big CTC number. Then someone mentions "Old Regime" and "New Regime" and suddenly your brain stops working.

**This calculator fixes that.**

Type in your salary. Get a plain-English answer in seconds — no CA needed, no Excel juggling, no YouTube rabbit hole at 2am.

---

## ✨ Two Modes — Because Not Everyone is the Same

### 💡 Simple Mode *(for freshers & Gen Z)*
- Just 5 inputs — salary, basic, city, HRA, rent
- Get a **WhatsApp-style result**: *"Hey Roop! Switch to New Regime and save ₹7,200/month 🎉"*
- Zero-tax alert if you're under ₹12.75L
- No jargon. No forms. Done in 30 seconds.

### 📊 Detailed Mode *(for the Excel nerds & finance folks)*
- Full Old vs New Regime comparison
- HRA with city-wise rules (yes, Bangalore is now metro 👀)
- FBP — all 10 components (Food Wallet, Mobile, NPS, Car Lease, LTA...)
- EPF (₹1,800 flat or 12% of basic), VPF, Gratuity
- Surcharge, Marginal Relief, AMT — all computed
- Capital Gains (LTCG, STCG — post Budget 2024 rates)
- TDS tracker — know your refund or balance due
- Scenario A vs B comparison
- Monthly in-hand salary for both regimes
- Print / PDF ready
